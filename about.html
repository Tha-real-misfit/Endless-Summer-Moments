A little about me :)
After working in the same industry, dental nursing, for 16 years and, after some major life experiences, I decided to follow a passion I have for capturing moments. I have now studied certificates, short courses and read as much as I can about photography to learn and grow into the photographer I am today. I hope to continue growing and learning more as the industry evolves. My passion is Macro Photography, capturing those little beauties of nature that we miss everyday. In general though, I just love capturing moments and love that I have that flexibility in my Photography knowledge to broaden my horizons and not be stuck to just one genre. Contact me if you would like me to capture a moment for you!
